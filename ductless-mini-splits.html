The Benefits of Ductless Mini-Splits

Mini splits are HVAC units perfect for small, media, sunrooms, and anywhere you don't want to add ductwork. A mini-split system is more affordable than a traditional central heating and cooling system and only a little higher cost than a cooling window unit. Ductless mini-split systems are highly efficient and act as an asset that will pay off through lower utility bills. You will also want to occasionally maintain your ductless system to run correctly for years to come. Tru Air offers complete repairs, maintenance, and ductless mini-split systems installation services.
