Ductless mini-splits

Mini splits are HVAC units perfect for small, media, sunrooms, and anywhere you don't want to add ductwork. A mini-split system is more affordable than a traditional central heating and cooling system and only a little higher cost than a cooling window unit. Ductless mini-split systems are highly efficient and act as an asset that will pay off through lower utility bills.
